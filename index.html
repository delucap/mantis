01 — Research Pillars

Three Perspectives,
One Conversation

Each seminar session is rooted in one of three interconnected research areas. Together, they define the intellectual landscape of MANTIS.

PILLAR 01

Machine Learning & Data-Driven Models

Learning from data, informed by physics — methods that bridge empirical evidence and mathematical structure.

PINNs Kernel Methods Operator Learning Bayesian Inference XAI

PILLAR 02

Numerical Analysis & Computational Methods

From differential equations to high-performance solvers — the mathematical foundations that underpin modern scientific computing.

PDEs Fractional Calculus HPC GPU Computing

PILLAR 03

Generative AI & Deep Architectures

Neural architectures that create, generate, and transform — from foundational models to scientific applications.

KANs Transformers Diffusion Models LLMs Transfer Learning

04 — About

About MANTIS

The MANTIS Seminar Series was established in 2026 at the Università degli Studi di Napoli "Parthenope" with the aim of creating a regular, open forum for the discussion of recent advances in numerical analysis, machine learning, and generative artificial intelligence.

The series is built around three equal pillars — computational mathematics, data-driven learning, and generative deep architectures — reflecting the increasingly intertwined nature of these disciplines in modern scientific research.

Each session features an invited talk followed by open discussion, and is designed to be accessible to a broad scientific audience, including PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, and faculty members from mathematics, computer science, and engineering.

We welcome proposals from researchers at all career stages. If you are interested in presenting your work, please get in touch.
